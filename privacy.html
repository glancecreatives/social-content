Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-24

1. Overview

Social Content is a private, self-use application designed to upload and schedule content to Meta platforms (Instagram, Facebook). It is not intended for public use or for third parties.

2. Data Collection

The app only accesses data authorized by the owner’s personal Meta account, including:

  • Access tokens issued by Meta for authentication
  • Media files, captions, and scheduling metadata uploaded by the owner
  • Basic profile and account details (as required by Meta’s API)

3. Data Usage

All collected data is used solely for posting and scheduling personal content. No data is shared or transmitted to any external service beyond Meta’s API.

4. Data Retention

Temporary tokens and post data are stored only for the duration required to complete uploads or scheduling actions. All data can be deleted manually by the owner at any time.

5. Ownership and Scope

This app is owned and operated by a single individual. It is not publicly distributed, not monetized, and not available to external users.
